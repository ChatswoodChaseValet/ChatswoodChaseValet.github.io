## Slide 2 — The valet, at a glance
- One cloud system shared live across the counter PC, tablets and staff phones
- Every car tracked drop-off → pickup; works offline, syncs automatically
- Branded shopper touchpoints: QR ticket, SMS updates, key-tag printing
- Card-first payments (Square terminal), with cash and account billing supported

*Speaker notes:* Emphasise reliability and that nothing is on paper — it's all auditable and real-time.
*Visual:* Three device mock-ups (phone, tablet, PC) showing the same dashboard. (Use the dashboard screenshot.)

## Slide 3 — The shopper journey
1. **Drop off** — logged in seconds; customer texted their QR ticket
2. **Shop** — hands-free: we can collect bags and add a car wash
3. **Request** — by phone, in person, or from their ticket page
4. **Pickup** — car ready and waiting; pay once for everything

*Speaker notes:* The experience is the product. Frictionless in, frictionless out.
*Visual:* A simple 4-step horizontal journey graphic with icons.

## Slide 9 — Added services (incremental value)
- **Hands-free shopping** — bags collected from stores, loaded into the car
- **Car wash concierge** — Xtream wash added to the valet bill; we handle transport both ways
- Both lift the experience *and* generate incremental revenue/margin

*Speaker notes:* Position these as differentiators that a standard car park can't offer.
*Visual:* Split layout — bag icon + car-wash droplet, with a "one bill" callout.

## Slide 10 — Risk & accountability
- **Damage register:** pre-existing damage marked at intake; new damage logged at handover with customer acknowledgement
- **Lost-ticket release:** identity verified and logged before any car is released
- **Overstay / overnight alerts:** long-staying and after-hours cars flagged
- Full **audit log** of who did what

*Speaker notes:* This is the risk/insurance story — protects the centre and the operator from disputes.
*Visual:* The **Incidents** report panel or the Active Cars overstay banner.

## Slide 11 — Data & privacy
- We hold shopper contact details to deliver the service (name, mobile, plate)
- Staff access is gated; password hashes are protected server-side
- **Roadmap:** lock customer data at the database layer + auto-purge old records, to meet Privacy Act obligations

*Speaker notes:* Be upfront — name the privacy commitment and the planned hardening. Management's risk team will appreciate candour.
*Visual:* A simple lock/shield graphic; keep it clean.
