**Valet Staff Training**
Running the Chatswood Chase valet, step by step

## Slide 2 — The golden rule
**Log the car the moment you take the keys. Mark it out the moment it drives away.**
- Every report, fee and "cars on site" count depends on your timing
- When in doubt, log it — accuracy protects you and the customer

*Speaker notes:* This single habit drives data accuracy. Reinforce it all day.
*Visual:* Big bold text, minimal — make it memorable.

---

## Slide 3 — Getting started
- Sign in with **your own login** (it credits your work)
- **Mobile mode** on a phone (fewer tabs); **Desktop mode** on the counter PC
- Turn on **keep-screen-awake** on the counter tablet

*Speaker notes:* Explain why individual logins matter (staff performance + accountability).
*Visual:* Login screen + the mobile/desktop toggle.

---

## Slide 4 — Checking a car IN
- New Entry → fill **Customer, Phone, Brand, Bay, Plate** (required)
- Plate can be **scanned** with the camera
- On Save: **key tag prints**, customer is **texted their QR ticket**, car appears on Active Cars

*Speaker notes:* Walk through the form live if you can. Stress Bay accuracy — it's how you find the car.
*Visual:* The **New Entry** screenshot (`screenshots/manual/new-entry.png`).

---

## Slide 5 — Mark damage BEFORE you park
- Tap the car diagram to mark any existing scratches/dents
- Shown again at checkout to verify the car is returned the same
- **This is your protection against false claims**

*Speaker notes:* Make this non-negotiable for any visible damage. 30 seconds now saves a dispute later.
*Visual:* Close-up of the damage diagram with a couple of marks.

---

## Slide 6 — Active Cars (your worklist)
- Every car on site: bay, time in, duration, running fee
- Duration colour: green <2h · amber 2–4h · red 4h+ · flashing 6h+
- Action icons: **call · QR · print · edit · bag · car wash**, then **Request** and **✓ Mark Out**

*Speaker notes:* This is home base. Point out the grouped icons vs the two big buttons.
*Visual:* The **Active Cars** screenshot (`screenshots/manual/active-cars.png`).

---

## Slide 7 — Overstay & overnight alerts
- A banner flags cars left **overnight** or **over [6] hours**
- Tap a flagged car's chip to jump straight to checkout
- Chase these early — they're the ones that cause problems

*Speaker notes:* Explain the threshold is set in Settings; overnight = sat through closing.
*Visual:* The overstay banner (red box with car chips).

---

## Slide 8 — Customer wants their car
- **Quick Pickup:** scan their QR / key tag, or search name, plate, bay
- **Queue + ETA:** when they ask ahead, tap **Request** and enter minutes
- The queue counts down and turns red when it's due

*Speaker notes:* Show both the instant find and the scheduled pickup. The runner works the queue.
*Visual:* The **Quick Pickup** screenshot (`screenshots/manual/quick-pickup.png`).

---

## Slide 9 — Lost ticket? Stay calm
1. Quick Pickup → **🎫 Lost ticket**
2. Search by name / plate / phone
3. **Verify identity** — match two details or check photo ID
4. Release — it's logged against your login

*Speaker notes:* Emphasise verification. Never release on a name alone.
*Visual:* The Lost-ticket modal.

---

## Slide 10 — Checking a car OUT
- Tap **✓ Mark Out** → review the fee (parking + $20 valet)
- Add any **validation / discount**; choose **payment** (Card is normal)
- **Return condition:** "No new damage" or log new damage + customer acknowledges
- **Square Terminal:** charge to the card machine; auto-checks-out on success

*Speaker notes:* Walk the whole modal. Stress the return-condition step — always check the car.
*Visual:* Checkout modal; callout on the Return condition toggle.

---

## Slide 11 — Hands-free shopping (bags)
- Log the pickup: store/location + number of bags
- Track it: **requested → collecting → in car → done**
- **Claim or assign:** tap **I'll get it**, or (desktop, at the counter) use **Assign to…** to give the job to a specific person — their phone **rings with an "Assigned to you" alert** and it shows as **YOUR JOB** on their screen
- **In car:** take one proof photo when you load the bags (this is sent to the customer)
- You're credited for the job

*Speaker notes:* Explain the customer leaves purchases with stores; we bring them to the car. For now there's just one photo — taken in the car as the customer's confirmation. (An in-shop photo step exists in the app but is switched off until we have each shop's permission to photograph in-store.)
*Visual:* The **Bags** screenshot (`screenshots/manual/bags.png`).

---

## Slide 12 — Car wash
1. **Add** the wash (check-in or the droplet on Active Cars) — pick service + size
2. **Taken to car wash** when you drive it to Xtream
3. **✓ Back from wash** when it returns
4. Customer **pays on the same bill** at checkout

*Speaker notes:* The chip turns maroon while it's "at car wash" — never hand over a car that's still away.
*Visual:* The car-wash manage dialog + the status chip on a row.

---

## Slide 13 — End of shift
- **Cash-up:** check the drawer against Reports → Payments
- **Overnight cars:** confirm what's staying and why
- **Handover notes:** write anything the next shift needs (VIP at 6pm, Bay 1 reserved…)

*Speaker notes:* A clean handover is part of the job. Notes auto-clear next morning.
*Visual:* Split: cash-up cards + the handover note widget.

---

## Slide 14 — Quick reference: do's & don'ts
**Do:** log immediately · mark damage · verify ID for lost tickets · keep bays accurate · check the car at return
**Don't:** release without a ticket unverified · hand over a car that's still at the wash · leave overstays unchecked · forget your own login

*Speaker notes:* End on the habits. Open the floor for questions and a hands-on practice run.
*Visual:* Two columns, green ticks / red crosses. Friendly closing line + "Questions?"
